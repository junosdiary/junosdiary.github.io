An Awkward day

March 1, 2025 · Juno

Well, this was an awkward day. I didn’t think this small incident would worth its own journal page, but why not.

Just as usual, Dad would give me a bath and leave me alone to play with water for 30 minutes. However, I was having such an amazing time and forgot how long it’s been. It must have been hours sitting in that bath tub and still not felt bored.

Just as I was having a blast, Dad decided to drain the water and let me sitting in an empty tub on the floor !! Fine, I couldn’t take it and decided it was time to get out, but somehow I needed to use the bathroom (#2 btw) just as I was standing up. Boy that felt good, but when I saw my own poop, I can’t help but screamed and started crying. Frankly, I don’t think I’ve really seen my own poop before and it was quite a scene !! I couldn’t believe my eyes and seeing that kind of stuff coming out of my bottom is unspeakable !!

Well, I didn’t think I was gonna have such a big reaction. Now I feel bad for Dad and Mom for taking care me everyday and doing these unspeakable things! No, not really feeling bad at all, that was just a joke.
