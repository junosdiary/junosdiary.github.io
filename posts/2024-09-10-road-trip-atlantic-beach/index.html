Road Trip to Atlantic Beach

September 10, 2024 · Juno

I honestly think that Dad and Mom were more excited than I am going to the beach. We booked a small room from Airbnb with 1 bedroom and a small kitchen. Somehow I ended up sleeping on the floor, yes, on the floor !! Can you imagine being a baby, sleeping on the cold and hard floor all alone ? It’s ridiculous ! So I decided to cry all night, which is how I always get my way and it worked! Mom and Dad finally agreed to put me in bed with them, and I slept really well for all those nights, though not so much for them. But who cares, the trip was to make me happy anyways

The location of the Airbnb room was fantastic. It was just a few walks away from the beach, weather was OK, I think only 1 day was sunny enough to stay by the beach, the other days were either too windy or too raining. Either way, I didn’t care, because it turns out that I don’t like the beach ! I am not a fan of sand, I just don’t like the tingling feelings and I refuse to put my feet on the beach or I start crying immediately !!

I heard and saw Mom trying to learn swimming; Dad was such a patient teacher, I adore him. They wanted me to learn swimming as well, and it was my first time putting a swim suit. The first day was fine, and watching Mom learning swimming isn’t as bad, after all she’s just as good as me. But 1 day is all I can stand, I refuse watching her learning every day, it’s just a very boring thing to do at the beach !!

The last day was great ! We had dinner at Amos Mosquito’s, they had amazing seafood, I enjoyed it there though I forgot what I had, but it was great for sure because I overheard Dad that the total was $100, so it must be good food !
